What we do not do

  • We do not use analytics or third-party trackers.
  • We do not load unnecessary external scripts.
  • We do not store your family details, estate values, or results remotely.
  • We do not require - or ask for - your name, email, or any account information.

What is stored, and where

While you are using the guided wizard, your in-progress answers are kept in your browser's sessionStorage so that reloading the page does not lose your progress. This data never leaves your device and is cleared automatically when you close the browser tab, or immediately when you choose "Clear Calculation".

Fonts are self-hosted with the application bundle rather than loaded from a third-party font service, so simply loading the page does not contact an external font provider either.
